Comprehensive Five-Pillar Strategic Framework
The ANIMO Health Technology and Innovation Center masterplan (2026-2030) establishes DLSMHSI as a premier Lasallian Global Health Innovation Hub. Through five interdependent strategic pillars, this comprehensive framework transforms research outputs into scalable health solutions, integrating Lasallian values with national priorities (NUHRA, RURHA, DOST-HNRDA) and global standards (WHO, UNESCO, WIPO, SDGs). From conceptual foundations to worldwide impact, each pillar ensures ethical, scientifically robust, and commercially viable innovations.
